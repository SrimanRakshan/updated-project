Indian Gate

The India Gate is a war memorial located astride the Rajpath, on the eastern edge of the "ceremonial axis" of New Delhi, formerly called Kingsway.

Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a region administered by India as a union territory and consisting of the southern portion of the larger Kashmir region.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal was commissioned by Shah Jahan in 1631, to be built in the memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is located in Agra, nearby Delhi. It is one of the greatest tourist spot.

Andaman Nicobar

The Andaman Islands are an Indian archipelago in the Bay of Bengal. These roughly 300 islands are known for their palm-lined, white-sand beaches, mangroves and tropical rainforests.
